
## 🎯 Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive **People Analytics Business Case** designed to identify the root causes of employee turnover (attrition) and validate corporate internal equity. Using a data-driven scientific approach, I translated raw HR data into actionable strategic recommendations.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* **The 1.9-Year Threshold:** High-potential talent decides to exit just before the 2-year mark. We recommend standardizing career path discussions at the **18-month milestone**.
* **Segmented Retention:** While entry-level turnover is driven by a **15-20% pay gap**, leadership attrition is linked to culture and autonomy, as leavers earned **$1,000/month MORE** than those who stayed.
* **Proven Internal Equity:** Statistical analysis confirmed virtually identical pay between genders in core technical roles, serving as a powerful **Employer Branding asset**.
* **Commute Resilience:** Data debunked the "commute tax" myth; environmental satisfaction remains stable regardless of distance, proving office quality outweighs the travel burden.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Decisions
* **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Uncovered hidden patterns across 35+ variables.
* **Data Cleaning:** Managed categorical encodings and addressed skewed distributions in `MonthlyIncome` using **Boxplots** to avoid "average" bias.
* **Visualization:** High-fidelity plotting with **Seaborn** and **Matplotlib** to ensure statistical significance and clarity for senior stakeholders.
